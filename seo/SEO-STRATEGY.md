## Key SEO Insights

- **Target UK market first** — Lanzarote is overwhelmingly a British holiday destination (33,100 searches/mo for "lanzarote holiday" from UK alone)
- **"lanzarote hidden gems" is the golden keyword** — 110 searches/mo, LOW competition, perfect brand fit
- **Wine keywords are strong** — "lanzarote wine tour" (480) + "lanzarote wine tasting" (320) = 800 combined, directly matching bodega experience
- **Commercial intent keywords to prioritise:** excursions, wine tour, boat trip, catamaran, fishing, day trips — people ready to book
- **Volcano tour content is a must** — 1,000 searches/mo with only KD 5 (very easy to rank)
